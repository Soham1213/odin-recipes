Little Nero's Cheese Pizza

Description

The smoky cheese blend provides a distinc flavor reminiscent of a wood-fired pie from a standard in-home oven and the pre-heated cooking surface doubles down on the difficult-to-attain-at-home crusty crunch.

Prep time: 10 mins

Cook time: 15 mins

Total time: 25 mins

Servings: 6

Yeild: 6 slices

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package prepared pizza dough
  • cornmeal
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded low-moisture mozzarella cheese
  • 1/3 cup shredded smoked Gouda cheese
  • 1/3 cup shredded smoked Jarlsberg cheese
  • 1/3 cup shaed Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded fresh basil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F with a pizza stone or inverted sheet tray on the middle rack.
  2. Allow dough to come to room temerature, covered, on the counter.
  3. Lightly dust a pizza peel or inverted sheet tray with cornmeal. Roll dough into a 16-inch circle on top. Top dough with marinara sauce, mozzarella, Gouda, Jarslberg, Parmesan, and basil. Transfer pizza directly on to the preheated stone.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until preferred level of browning is attained, 14 to 16 minutes. Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing into 6 slices.
