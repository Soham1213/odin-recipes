Southern Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Very stick, southern-style macaroni and cheese

Prep time: 10 mins

Cook time: 50 mins

Total time: 1 hr

Servings: 10

Yeild: 10 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 3 (8 ounce) containers cottage cheese
  • 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9x13 baking dish
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. IN a large bowl comine cooked pasta, cottage cheese, sour cream, eggs, Cheddar cheese, and cayenne pepper. Mix well and transfer to prepared dish. Sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Cover loosely with aluminum foil and bake 40 minutes.
